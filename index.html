About Us


K.K. IMPEX is a Jaipur based trusted firm that is engaged in the supplying of a variety of industrial fasteners and fittings. We are continuing to flourish at a constant rate under the efficient guidance of our worthy Proprietor , Mr. Darshan Khandelwal.
